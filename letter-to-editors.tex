\noindent Dear Editors of JCP,

\vspace{1cm}

This submission is of the revised manuscript for \mbox{JCP19-AR-EISC2019-03459}. We apologize for the delay in resubmitting. 

We thank the referees for taking the time to review our manuscript. In response we have made the following changes to the paper: first, we have added a section that describes in more detail the models that we use and why we, and others in the literature~\cite{Barato2017}, believe them to be good representations of oscillator dynamics for our purposes. Second, we went through and clarified terms which we realize were poorly defined, such as ``robustness". In a separate letter we have provided detailed responses to each of the referees' comments. We hope that with these changes the work can be accepted for publication in JCP.

\vspace{1cm}
\noindent Regards,
